Quick Start Guide

Step 1: Input Your Overdue Invoices

Enter the client name, original invoice amount, and the date it was originally due. Our engine will instantly calculate the days late and the legal interest owed to you.

Step 2: Generate Escalation Emails

Click "Get Email" next to any invoice. Our system will generate a professional but firm escalation message including the new total with late fees added.

Step 3: Collect Your Cash

Copy the email, paste it into your email provider, and send. Watch your bank account fill up with found profit.
